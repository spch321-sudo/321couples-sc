Group Follow-up · Seven Gatherings

Bring the camp's lessons back to your small group—seven gatherings of two hours each, led by the host couple, helping every couple put each topic's truth into practice in daily life.

Every gathering follows a set rhythm: Welcome & icebreaker → Worship & declaration → Review last week → Revisit the truth → couple practice (the heart) → Group sharing → Commitment & prayer. The point isn't hearing more, but each couple practicing on the spot and living it out at home.
"For where two or three gather in my name, there am I with them." (Matthew 18:20)
